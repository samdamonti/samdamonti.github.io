NUROVER

I'm a current software co-lead for Northeastern's Mars Rover team. The team competes with our beloved rover Watney Mark V in the University Rover Challenge (URC) in Hankesville, Utah and in Winter 2024 at the Canadian International Rover Challenge. The team is a group of multidisciplinary engineers, scientists, and creators who share a love for robotics, capybaras, and soup.

Drepeater

Drepeater (Drone Repeater) was a project for the 2024 URC Extreme Delivery Challenge as a possible solution to our issue with communication loss beyond line of sight. In collaboration with Northeastern's NUAV club we used a drone to relay simple drive commands to the rover.
